Paella

Description

There are few people who do not fall for the Valencian paella. Paella is probably the most acclaimed, popular and well-known recipe of Spanish gastronomy. It is the flagship dish of an entire region, which has allowed the Spanish culinary richness to transcend borders.

Learn how to prepare the famous Valencian paella and follow our step-by-step recipe to make it perfect. Take note of the ingredients and follow our step by step recipe to prepare an authentic paella recipe.

Ingredients

  • 400 g bomba rice
  • 1 kg chopped chicken
  • 500 g chopped rabbit
  • 200 g green beans
  • 100 g fresh lima beans
  • 100 g chopped tomato
  • 6 tablespoons of olive oil
  • A few strands of saffron
  • Ground red paprika
  • A sprig of rosemary
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the oil over the paella pan and turn on the heat. Once the oil is hot, add the rabbit and the chicken and fry until golden brown.
  2. Remember to turn the pieces of meat so that they cook evenly. Then, add the vegetables and sauté them.
  3. Now pour the grated tomato and sauté it in the same way, stirring constantly.
  4. Now it is the turn of the paprika. Add it and stir it just for a moment, then you have to pour the water up to the rivets of the paella to prevent it from burning. Add the saffron, the rosemary and the salt.
  5. Once the water comes to a boil, let it boil for about 5 minutes over high heat. Then, lower the heat to medium and let it cook for 20 to 30 minutes. Increase the heat and add the rice.
  6. The traditional way to put it is to make a cross in the boiling water and spread it evenly over the surface of the pot. Take advantage of this time to taste and correct the salt.
  7. Keep the fire high for about 10 minutes and reduce the fire to half for a similar time until the rice is cooked. Enjoy!
